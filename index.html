Our story

Born slow,
on purpose.

Slothie started on a slow Tuesday morning in the cloud forests of Costa Rica. Our founder, tired of rushing everywhere and drinking terrible smoothies at airport terminals, decided something had to change.

The idea was simple: what if a smoothie company operated the way a sloth moves — deliberately, unhurriedly, and with complete confidence that it would get there eventually?

We source only from small farms, blend in small batches, and refuse to add anything we can't pronounce. No shortcuts. No artificial rush. Just fruit, done properly.
